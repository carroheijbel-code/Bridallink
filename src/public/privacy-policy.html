Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Effective Date: December 2024

At BridalLink, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our wedding planning platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, phone number
  • Profile Information: Wedding date, venue, partner details, preferences
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Communication Data: Messages, consultation requests, support communications
  • Wedding Planning Data: Budget information, guest lists, vendor details, timeline information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser information
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, referring websites
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience

1.3 Third-Party Information

  • Social Media: Information from social media platforms if you connect your accounts
  • Vendor Information: Details shared by wedding vendors or service providers
  • Community Content: Posts, photos, and interactions in our bride community

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Provide and maintain our wedding planning platform
  • Process your registration and manage your account
  • Enable budget tracking, guest management, and timeline planning
  • Facilitate document storage and organization
  • Connect you with our expert wedding consultants (Premium members)

2.2 Premium Services

  • Provide expert wedding consultations with Carolina, our Mallorca specialist
  • Offer Spanish translation and cultural guidance services
  • Connect you with trusted vendor networks
  • Provide priority customer support via WhatsApp and email

2.3 Community Features

  • Enable participation in our bride community forums
  • Facilitate photo sharing and experience discussions
  • Provide peer support and advice networks
  • Moderate content to ensure a safe, supportive environment

2.4 Business Operations

  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service-related communications and updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our services through analytics and user feedback
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Payment Processing & Stripe Integration

3.1 Stripe Payment Processing

We use Stripe, Inc. as our payment processor for all premium subscription transactions. When you make a payment:

  • Your payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe using industry-standard encryption
  • We do not store your complete credit card information on our servers
  • Stripe maintains PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, the highest level of payment security
  • We store only the last four digits of your card and the card type for identification purposes

3.2 Subscription Management

  • We process recurring payments for monthly (£4.99) and yearly (£49.99) premium subscriptions
  • All payments are securely processed through Stripe's encrypted payment infrastructure
  • We maintain records of subscription status and billing history for account management
  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings

3.3 Refund Policy

  • We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new premium subscriptions
  • Refund requests are processed through Stripe's secure refund system
  • Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (under strict PCI compliance)
  • Supabase: Database hosting and backend services
  • Email Services: For sending transactional emails and notifications
  • Analytics Providers: To understand and improve our service (anonymized data)

4.3 Expert Consultants

For Premium members receiving consultation services:

  • Relevant wedding planning information may be shared with Carolina, our Mallorca specialist
  • This enables personalized advice and vendor recommendations
  • All consultants are bound by confidentiality agreements

4.4 Community Features

When you participate in our bride community:

  • Your name and posts are visible to other community members
  • Photos and experiences you share become part of the community content
  • You control what information you choose to share publicly

4.5 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms of service

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are regularly reviewed and updated

5.2 Payment Security

  • All payment processing meets PCI DSS standards through Stripe
  • We do not store complete payment card information
  • Payment data is tokenized and encrypted at all times

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Account Access and Control

  • Access: View and download your personal information
  • Update: Modify your account information and preferences
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and personal information
  • Export: Download your wedding planning data

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional emails (service emails will continue)
  • Manage notification preferences in your account settings
  • Control community participation and visibility

6.3 Subscription Management

  • Cancel premium subscriptions at any time
  • Access billing history and payment information
  • Request refunds within our 30-day guarantee period

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and support your wedding planning
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes
  • Improve our services and prevent fraud

You may request deletion of your account at any time. Upon deletion:

  • Your personal information will be permanently removed
  • Community posts may remain but will be anonymized
  • Billing records may be retained for legal and tax purposes

8. Children's Privacy

BridalLink is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete such information promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and European Union. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our platform
  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address
  • Providing notice through our platform interface

Your continued use of BridalLink after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
